About Mike's Broilers

Mike's Broilers is a family-owned business dedicated to providing the best quality broilers. With years of experience and a passion for poultry, Mike ensures that every broiler is raised in optimal conditions to deliver fresh, healthy, and delicious products to our customers.

Our Mission

To provide our customers with top-quality broilers that are healthy, affordable, and delivered with exceptional service.

Our Story

Started in 2010, Mike's Broilers began as a small farm with a big dream. Over the years, we have grown into a trusted name in the community, known for our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.
